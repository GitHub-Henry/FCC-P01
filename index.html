Benjamin Franklin

Man of Many Talents

Newspaperman, Inventor, Scientist, Politician

Events in the life of Benjamin Franklin

  • 1728 - Franklin had set up a printing house in partnership with Hugh Meredith.
  • 1720 - Became publisher of the Pennsylvania Gazzette
  • 1732 - Franklin published the first German language newspaper in America
  • 1746 - Franklin started exploring the phenomenon of electricity
  • 1750 - Franklin published a proposal to prove that lightning is electricity
  • 1753 - Franklin received the Royal Society's Copley Medal
  • 1770 - Franklin published his Gulf Stream chart
  • 1786 - Philosophical Society's published Franklin's oceanographic findings
  • 1748 - Franklin was selected as a councilman
  • 1749 - He became a Justice of the Peace for Philadelphia
  • 1751 - He was elected to the Pennsylvania Assembly
  • 1754 - He headed the Pennsylvania delegation to the Albany Congress
  • 1756 - Franklin organized the Pennsylvania Militia
  • 1765 - Franklin opposed the 1765 Stamp Act
